tasmeem

A simple CSS framework. This page previews the built stylesheet.

See the buttons showcase and the cards showcase for every component case.

Components are neutral by default; compose a variant (meaning) with a style (appearance) by class — btn primary, btn danger outline, btn success ghost.

Card surface

Background, border, and shadow are all derived from the surface and shadow base tokens.

A themed link uses the primary token.

Muted text derives its contrast from the surface too.

Edit the partials in src/css/ and rebuild.
