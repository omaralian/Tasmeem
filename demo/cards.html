Cards

The card is a surface container. As with buttons, intent (meaning) and style (appearance) are independent classes that compose: card + an intent (primary, danger, …) + a style (outline, subtle, accent). Lay out content with card-header, card-body, and card-footer. Build the stylesheet, then toggle the theme above to verify each case in light and dark.

Anatomy

A card clips its corners and carries no padding of its own; sections provide it. card-header and card-footer add a divider; card-body grows to fill the height.

With header & footer

The body holds the main content and stretches so footers line up across a row of cards.
